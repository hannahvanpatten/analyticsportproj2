# Demand Forecasting: A Case Study
This repo contains the code for my second data analytics portfolio project.


## Project overview
This project will develop a demand forecasting and inventory optimization system for a healthcare manufacturer that supplies medicinal products to hospitals. Using 36 months of historical sales and inventory data across 250 products, I will build predictive models to forecast future demand and generate inventory planning recommendations based on lead times, safety stock requirements, storage costs, and product shelf-life constraints. The analysis will evaluate whether a data-driven approach can improve upon traditional manual forecasting methods by reducing inventory carrying costs, minimizing stockout risk, and supporting more efficient production planning. The project combines time-series forecasting, inventory analytics, financial modeling, and business intelligence to demonstrate how analytics can drive operational decision-making and improve business outcomes.


## Business problem
A manufacturer of medicinal products serving the hospital healthcare market currently relies on manually generated sales forecasts to support production and inventory decisions. Forecasting errors can result in excess inventory, increased storage costs, product expiration, stockouts, and lost sales. Because medicinal products have limited shelf lives and production lead times, inaccurate forecasts create significant operational and financial risk. The organization needs a data-driven forecasting and inventory optimization system that improves demand prediction accuracy and supports more efficient inventory planning.


## Dataset description
financial_plan.csv contains the unit selling price for each product, the planned sales for 2023-2025, and the planned revenue for 2023-2025.

products_parameters.csv contains the storage cost per unit, product expiration horizon, time required to replenish inventory, required inventory buffer, and selling price for each product.

sales_train_2023_2024.csv contains the sales and inventory levels for each product and month in 2023 and 2024.

sales_test_2025.csv contains the sales and inventory levels for each product and month in 2025.


## Methodology
To address the company's forecasting and inventory management challenges, I will first consolidate historical sales, inventory, product parameter, and financial planning data into a unified analytical dataset. I will then perform exploratory data analysis to identify demand patterns, seasonality, inventory trends, and operational constraints across 250 products. Using 24 months of historical data (2023–2024), I will develop and evaluate a demand forecasting model against actual 2025 outcomes to determine the most accurate approach. Forecast outputs will then be integrated with product-specific lead times, safety stock requirements, storage costs, and shelf-life constraints to generate inventory and production planning recommendations. Finally, I will quantify the potential business impact of the proposed system by comparing forecast-driven decisions against the company's existing planning process, with a focus on forecast accuracy, inventory carrying costs, stockout risk, and potential revenue protection.
